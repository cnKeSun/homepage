Ke Sun | INRIA | Biography

IEEE Style Short Bio

Dr. Ke Sun (S’15 - M’20) received the Ph.D degree in Automatic Control and Systems Engineering (ACSE) from University of Sheffield, Sheffield, UK, in 2020, the first M.Sc. degree in Control Theory and Control Engineering from Tongji University, Shanghai, China, in 2015, the second M.Sc. degree in Automation Engineering from the University of Bologna, Bologna, Italy, in 2015, and the B.Eng. degree in Automation from the Shandong University of Science and Technology, Qingdao, China, in 2012. He is currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in Centre de Recherche de Sophia Antipolis - Méditerranée, INRIA, Sophia Antipolis, France. He was Postdoctoral Research Associate in University of Sheffield between 2019 and 2021. Recognition of his work includes the 2020 ACSE prize and the Honorable Mention Award within the Engineering Science Category in the 2020 Doctoral Research Award.

His research interests include the security of cyber-physical systems, information theory, random matrix theory, and game theory. He serves as a Reviewer for several journals and conferences, including IET Smart Grid, IEEE Internet Things J., IEEE Trans. Ind. Informat., and IET Cyber-Physical Systems: Theory & Applications.
