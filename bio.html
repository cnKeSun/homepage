Ke Sun | Shanghai University | Biography

IEEE Style Short Bio

Dr. Ke Sun (S’15 - M’20 - SM’24) received the Ph.D degree from University of Sheffield, Sheffield, UK, in 2020, the M.Sc. degree from Tongji University, Shanghai, China, and the University of Bologna, Bologna, Italy, in 2015. He is currently a Lecturer in School of Computer Engineering and Science, Shanghai University, Shanghai, China. Prior to that, he was a Postdoctoral Researcher in INRIA Centre at Université Côte d’Azur, INRIA, Sophia Antipolis, France, in 2022, and a Postdoctoral Research Associate in University of Sheffield between 2019 and 2021. He is the recipient of the 2023 Shanghai Overseas Talents Program, the 2020 ACSE prize, and the Honorable Mention Award within the Engineering Science Category in the 2020 Doctoral Research Award.

His research interests include the security of cyber-physical systems. He serves as TPC member for IEEE SmartGridComm 2023&2024, and as a Reviewer for several journals and conferences, including IET Smart Grid, IEEE Internet Things J., IEEE Trans. Ind. Informat., and IET Cyber-Physical Systems: Theory & Applications.
